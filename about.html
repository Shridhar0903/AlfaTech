ABOUT ALFA TECH INDIA

Trusted Dairy Equipment Manufacturer Since 1991

Incepted in 1991, Alfa Tech India is a well-known manufacturer and exporter of dairy equipment including Milking Machines, Milk Analysers, Automatic Milk Collection Systems, Bulk Milk Coolers, Khoya Machines, Milk Cans, Cream Separators, Electronic Weighing Scales and Paneer Press.

Our products are highly durable, leak-proof and energy efficient, making them widely used in dairies and food industries across India. We manufacture all equipment using sturdy metal and advanced technology recommended by experienced mechanical engineers.

With modern infrastructure and a skilled team of engineers and food experts, we ensure high product accuracy, durability and reliability for every dairy solution we deliver.
