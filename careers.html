Open Positions at Alfa Tech

Service Engineer

Qualifications: 10th / 12th / ITI / Diploma pass with adequate knowledge of English.

Responsibilities: Travel to rural areas in India to install milking machines and other systems.

Senior R&D Engineer (Electronics)

Experience: Minimum 3 years.

Responsibilities:

  • Core electronics circuit designing for analogue & digital systems.
  • Work with Orcad or Allegro for schematic designing (layout optional).
  • Use of oscilloscope, function generators, and logic analysers.
  • Circuit tracing and knowledge of standard electronic circuits (op-amps, filters, comparators).
  • Familiarity with standard digital logic circuits.

Associate Programmer

Experience: 1–3 years.

Qualifications: BE (Computer/IT), BCS, or MCS.

Responsibilities:

  • Work on Windows Azure cloud.
  • Must know C++ & C#.
  • Knowledge of Windows internals, sockets, RPC (DCOM), and threading.
