About Us


Innovative Solutions for India's Dairy Industry

“Delivering reliable and advanced dairy technology since 1990.”

Established in 1991, Alfa Tech India is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of dairy equipment, offering products like Milking Machines, Milk Analysers, Automatic Milk Collection Systems, Bulk Milk Coolers, Khoya Machines, Milk Cans, Cream Separators, Electronic Weighing Scales, and Paneer Press.

Our products are durable, energy-efficient, and designed for safe, leak-free operation, making them widely used in dairies and food industries.

Today Alfa Tech India proudly serves more than 100,000 satisfied customers with reliable, customized dairy equipment supported by professional engineering assistance.
