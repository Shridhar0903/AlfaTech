Product Overview

Alfa Tech India Milking Machine ensures fast, hygienic, and efficient milk extraction. Built with durable components and advanced technology for modern dairy farms.

  • Milking time: 4–5 minutes per animal
  • Works for cows and buffaloes
  • Automatic cut-off system
  • No harm to animals
  • Easy installation & operation
  • Saves labor and time

Configurations

  • Mini: up to 5 cows/hour
  • Single Bucket: 15 cows / 10 buffaloes
  • Double Bucket: 30 cows / 25 buffaloes
  • Multi-Bucket: 50+ animals/hour

Technical Specifications

  • Motor: 0.25 / 0.5 / 1 HP
  • Vacuum Pump: 200 / 400 LPM
  • Bucket: 20L SS 304
  • Orbiter: 300cc
  • Pulsator: 60/40 ratio
  • Brush Set

Accessories

  • 1.5 HP Honda Engine (optional)
